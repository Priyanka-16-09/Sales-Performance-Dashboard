|| Sales Performance Dashboard ||

📌 1. Project Overview:-
This project analyzes sales data to track business performance and identify key trends in sales, profit, and growth.

🎯 2. Objective:-
Analyze sales and profit performance
Identify top-performing regions and products
Understand business trends
Provide insights for better decision-making

3. Tools Used:-
Excel – Data cleaning & pivot analysis
SQL (MySQL) – Data analysis
Power BI – Dashboard creation

4. Dataset:-
Superstore Sales Dataset (Kaggle)

5. Key Metrics:-
Overall revenue & profit
Region-wise performance
Category & product contribution
Customer performance
Impact of discount on profit
Monthly sales trend

6.Excel Analysis:-
Used pivot tables to analyze sales by region, category, and product
Performed initial data exploration and summary analysis

7. Analysis Performed:-
Sales and profit calculation using SQL
Region-wise and product-wise performance analysis
Identified high and low-performing products
Analyzed trends over time

8. Key Insights:-
Some regions generate high sales but low profit
Few products contribute most of the revenue
Discounts reduce overall profitability
Sales trends vary across different periods

9. Conclusion:-
This project helps understand sales performance and highlights areas to improve profitability and efficiency.
